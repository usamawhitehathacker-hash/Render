# 🎯 ORLAVEN — 25 UNIQUE HOMEPAGE & LANDING PAGE BLUEPRINTS
## Complete Detailed Structure Guide with ASCII Wireframes
### Based on Analysis of 200+ Premium Leather/Fashion Brand Websites

---

> **Brand Name:** ORLAVEN  
> **Niche:** Premium Leather Jackets, Bags, Boots & Accessories  
> **Target:** Men & Women (25-45) who value craftsmanship, style & authenticity  
> **Psychology Triggers Used:** Scarcity, Social Proof, Loss Aversion, FOMO, Authority, Reciprocity, Anchoring, Storytelling

---

## 🧠 HUMAN PSYCHOLOGY PRINCIPLES APPLIED IN ALL BLUEPRINTS

| Principle | How It Works | Where Applied |
|-----------|-------------|---------------|
| **Scarcity** | "Only 3 left" — makes item feel rare | Product cards, hero banners |
| **Social Proof** | "12,847 jackets sold" — others trust us | Trust bar, reviews section |
| **Loss Aversion** | People fear losing more than gaining | Limited drops, countdown timers |
| **FOMO** | Fear of Missing Out drives action | Flash sales, "Selling Fast" badges |
| **Authority** | Expert endorsement builds trust | Press logos, celebrity features |
| **Reciprocity** | Give free value, they feel obliged | Free style guide, free shipping |
| **Anchoring** | Show high price first, then discount | ~~$599~~ → $349 pricing |
| **Storytelling** | Emotional narratives create connection | Brand story, craftsman videos |
| **Commitment** | Small "yes" leads to bigger "yes" | Quiz → email → purchase |
| **Identity** | "For rebels who..." creates belonging | Tribe messaging throughout |
